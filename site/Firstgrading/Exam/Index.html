  1. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

    2. by: Sara Mason

    Source: https://medium.com/codex/

    Five top trends for 2021 in Artificial Intelligence and Machine
    Learning (AI/ML) for staying ahead of the curve. What is
    important for being effective for applying algorithms in coding
    to utilize data to solve problems?

    Blockchain, is a technology that uses a sequential set of
    transactions leaving a defined register of events and processfor
    combining tasks. Fusing AI/ML and blockchain for data
    solutions is coming.

    Cloud, is scalable and virtual environments. These are available
    to set users with no physical location or provision requirement.
    Cloud creates a different model for IT and data services by not
    restricting architecture by physical requirements.

    Ethical Artificial Intelligence, is a field, study, and
    implementation of framework for limiting or eliminating bias in
    algorithms. This involves evaluating a solution for data use and
    implementation for cultural and social impact.

    Interoperability, using APIs and libraries together through
    replication or scripting across stacks for easy implementations.
    Having reusable and expandable blocks for coding across
    platforms such as Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, SAP,
    Snowflake, and more.

    Security, keeping data safe through stewardship. Both innovation
    and workflow will be a topic for 2021 to enhance security
    methods for specialized data services based on Artificial
    Intelligence and Machine Learning to differentiate from software
    development norms.

    The way to stay ahead is to know the list of technologies that
    touch a domain and achieve proficiency in them for incorporation
    into tasks to maintain productivity. The five themes for 2021
    for maintaining relevance in AI/ML: Blockchain, Cloud, Ethical
    Artificial Intelligence (AI), Interoperability, and Security
    (Cybersecurity).

  2. Telehealth and telemedicine

    3. by Anis Uzzaman

    Source: https://www.inc.com/anis-uzzaman/top-business-technology-trends-in-2021

    Institutions, especially in health care, are working to lower
    the exposure of Covid-19 to patients and workers. Many private
    and public practices have started implementing more telehealth
    offerings such as doctor-patient video chats, A.I. avatar-based
    diagnostics, and no-contact-based medication delivery.

    Telehealth visits have surged by 50 percent compared with pre-
    pandemic levels. IHS Technology predicted that 70 million
    Americans would use telehealth by 2020. Since then, Forrester
    Research predicted the number of U.S. virtual care visits will
    reach almost a billion early in 2021.

    Teladoc Health, Amwell, Livongo Health, One Medical, and Humana
    are some of the public companies offering telehealth services to
    meet the current needs. Startups are not far behind. Startups
    like MDLive, MeMD, iCliniq, K Health, 98point6, Sense.ly, and
    Eden Health have also contributed toward meeting the growing
    needs in 2020, and will continue offering creative solutions in
    2021. Beyond telehealth, in 2021 we can expect to see health
    care advancements in biotech and A.I., as well as machine
    learning opportunities (example: Suki AI) to support diagnosis,
    admin work, and robotic health care.
